Asapio
Asapio is SAP, SAP Ariba and SAP Fieldglass Partner and specialized on
implementation of SAP Procurement Solutions like SAP SRM, MM, catalog
systems as well as Ariba and Fieldglass Cloud Solutions.
As to further main focuses, we implement SAP projects in the engineering
and automotive industry and carry out SAP-based development projects.
More about Asapio
Products and Solutions
The solutions are designed to increase the efficiency for using the SAP applications
and by this leverage the ROI of the customer's SAP implementation.
CVI for SAP UI5 / MDM
This solution is used for validating catalog
content. It can be adopted to every business
process that includes validation easily.
PD Monitor
The Procurement Documents Monitor
provides powerful reports for Workflow
Monitoring and value tracing.
Extended Workflow Inbox
You are looking for a multiclient inbox structered
by folders? Our Extended Workflow Inbox (XWI)
might be the solution you are looking for.
Bid Evaluation
The BID-Evaluation is another BAdI
Implementation (as the PDUD) that provides
an Microsoft Excel® Sheet with comparision
of all Quotations made by suppliers.
PD Up- and Download
The Purchasing Up- and Download tool
provides an Microsoft Excel® sheet to
down- and upload Purchase Orders,
Contracts, RFx and Quotations.
Item POWL
The Item POWL by Asapio extends SAP
POWL by all the items of the selected
shopping cart.
